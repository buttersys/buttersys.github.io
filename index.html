About Us

buttersys is a software company specializing in fleet simulation and AI. Our fleetsim product helps teach students software engineering skills within a challenging and exciting automation system comprised of a large mobile robot fleet. And in addition to education, fleetsim is used for research in robotic fleet AI and control.
